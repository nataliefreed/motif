This software is part of a research study at the University of Texas at Austin about digital and physical crafts. If you agree to participate, we will collect anonymous data about the way you use the software. Participation is optional, and you can still use the software without participating in the study. Click here to learn more and decide whether to participate.

If you agree to participate, we will collect the following data:

  • The designs you create in the software
  • The steps you used to create the designs
  • Any optional feedback you share in the comment box

This data will be collected and stored securely. However, there is always a risk of data online being exposed. Please do not put any personal information in your designs.

If it is published as part of the research, the data will be anonymous and not associated with your name or identity unless you request otherwise.
